About EcoRide

EcoRide is an innovative e-bike rental service based in Edinburgh, offering eco-friendly, affordable, and convenient travel options to residents, tourists, and daily commuters.

Our Services

We provide a fleet of modern, eco-friendly e-bikes, available for rental through our app, with intelligent docking stations powered by solar energy.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to transform urban transportation in Edinburgh by providing sustainable and affordable e-bike rentals, aligning with global climate action goals.
